Chocolate Cake

Submitted by: Madina Elysi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into greased baking pans.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool before serving. Enjoy!

Vegan Bowl

Submitted by: Madina Elyasi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Lemon juice, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to instructions.
  2. In a bowl, layer quinoa, beans, corn, tomatoes, and avocado.
  3. Season with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Serve chilled or warm.

Quick Pasta

Submitted by: Madina Elyas

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in pan, sauté garlic and chili flakes.
  3. Toss in pasta. Mix well.
  4. Sprinkle parmesan and serve.

Healthy Smoothie

Submitted by: Madina Elyasi

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy fresh.

Breakfast Omelette

Submitted by: Sunny Chef

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sauté veggies in butter.
  3. Pour eggs over veggies and cook until set.
  4. Fold and serve hot.
